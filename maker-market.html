Maker Market

The 2022 Moriah Maker Market

The Moriah Chamber of Commerce is pleased to once again host the Moriah Maker's Market, formerly known as "Homegrown In The Park". The change of name was driven by a desire to better reflect what our market is and celebrate those who make it all possible.

New for 2022 The Maker's Market will be held on Saturdays and is moving to a new, larger location on the Port Henry Waterfront at Bridgeview Harbour Marina. This new location offers additional space for our market to expand allowing us to accept more vendors. Additionally it is within easy walking distance of downtown as well as both town campgrounds; allowing campers, boaters, and anyone else easy access to the market.

Details of the market are still being worked out, so stay tuned to our Facebook Page and this site for more information in the coming months.

If you are interested in participating in this year's market as a vendor, performing musician, or volunteer, please fill out the form below and a member of the chamber staff will get back to you.
